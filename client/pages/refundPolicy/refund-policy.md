# Refund Policy

**Company:** Foxbiz Software Pvt. Ltd.  
**Effective Date:** March 11, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 16, 2026

---

## Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all purchases made on the Acode platform (acode.app), operated by **Foxbiz Software Pvt. Ltd.**, including **plugin purchases** and **Acode Pro purchases**.

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you are not completely satisfied, we are here to help.

---

## Self-Service Refunds (Razorpay Purchases)

All purchases made via Razorpay — including plugin purchases on the website and Acode Pro — are eligible for a **self-service refund within 2 hours** of purchase.

- **Plugin purchases:** A "Request Refund" button is available on the plugin page while you are within the refund window.
- **Acode Pro:** A "Request Refund" button is available on the [Pro page](/pro) while you are within the refund window.

Self-service refunds are processed automatically. No email or support ticket is required.

Once the 2-hour window has passed, self-service refunds are no longer available. You may still contact our support team for assistance (see below).

---

## Google Play Purchases

For purchases made through the Acode mobile app via Google Play, refunds are subject to **Google Play's refund policies**. Please request a refund through the Google Play Store or contact Google Play support directly.

---

## Manual Refund Requests

If you are outside the self-service refund window, you may still be eligible for a manual refund if:

- You were charged but did not receive access to the plugin or Acode Pro.
- The plugin was significantly different from its description on the listing page.
- You experienced a technical issue that prevented you from using the plugin or Acode Pro, and it could not be resolved within a reasonable time.
- You were accidentally charged more than once for the same purchase.

---

## Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will **not** be issued in the following cases:

- You changed your mind after purchasing (and the 2-hour self-service window has passed).
- You do not have the technical skills required to use the plugin.
- You purchased the plugin or Acode Pro accidentally but have already accessed or used it beyond the refund window.
- The refund request is made more than 7 days after the original purchase date.

---

## How to Request a Manual Refund

To request a manual refund (outside the 2-hour self-service window), please contact us at:

**Email:** [contact@acode.app](mailto:contact@acode.app)

Include the following details in your email:

- Your registered email address
- Whether this is a plugin purchase or Acode Pro purchase
- The name of the plugin (if applicable)
- The date of purchase
- The reason for your refund request
- Any relevant screenshots or transaction details

---

## Refund Processing

- **Self-service refunds** are initiated immediately. Please allow **5–7 business days** for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.
- **Manual refund requests** will be reviewed by our support team and responded to within **3–5 business days**. Approved refunds will be processed back to your original payment method. Please allow an additional **5–10 business days** for the refund to appear in your account.
